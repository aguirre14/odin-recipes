Old Fashioned

The Old Fashioned is a cocktail made by muddling sugar with bitters and water, adding whiskey, and garnishing with an orange slice or zest and a cocktail cherry. It is traditionally served with ice in an old fashioned glass.

In this version, we will be simplifying the making by using sugar syrup instead of sugar and water, and only an orange peel for garnishing

Ingredients

    60ml Bourbon
    10ml Sugar syrup
    8 dashes of Angostura Bitters
    Orange peel
